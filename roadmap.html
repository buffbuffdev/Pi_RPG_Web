Pi RPG | First Real-Yeld Game

Game Concept: Pi RPG is a pixel-art role-playing game mixing open-world exploration, tactical combat, and narrative depth. Every ability has a dual use, both in combat and in the environment.

World & Story: Start your journey in a mysterious world where every choice matters. Encounter unique characters, uncover hidden secrets, and face challenging puzzles that test your strategic thinking. Your actions shape the world and influence the story.

Exploration: Explore vast regions, unlock powerful abilities, and prepare for epic battles!

Community & Development: Pi RPG is currently a community-driven project in revenue sharing. It is not yet established as a legal entity and provides no guarantees of any kind. The game is developed by a small indie team passionate about retro gaming and pixel art. Contributors share potential profits based on their involvement.

Exploration: In Pi RPG, exploration is key. Discover hidden zones, unlock secrets, and interact with a living, breathing world that reacts to your choices.

Combat: Combat is tactical and challenging. Each ability can be used in multiple ways, both in battles and to solve environmental puzzles. Mastering these mechanics is essential for success.

Crafting & Resources: Gather materials, craft powerful items, and upgrade your gear to face tougher challenges.

Narrative: The game includes a rich story with branching paths. Your decisions affect character relationships, quests, and the world itself.

Multiplayer: Planned features include item trading, strategy sharing, and community events within the Pi RPG universe.

Overview: Pi RPG is split into two complementary experiences: the Game and the Tournament. These experiences are strictly separated to guarantee fairness and regulatory compliance.

The Game (Free-to-Play + In-Game Shop): Full gameplay with exploration, RNG elements, progression, and optional in-game purchases (cosmetics and convenience features). The Game does not award any financial rewards.

The Tournament (Competitive & Fair-play): Fully skill-based: no RNG, no influence from in-game progression or purchases. Entry is free. Tournament rewards are financial (crypto) and funded exclusively by interest/dividends generated through the Holding's RWA investments.

Goal: This separation ensures a fair competitive environment while preserving a sustainable, compliant P2E model.

Economic Model: Pi RPG plans a P2E economy where game revenues are invested in real-world assets to create value for the studio and share interest with the community for long-term engagement.

Structure: The project has a dual separation to ensure sustainability and compliance:

Two legal entities: The Studio develops and operates the game, while the Holding owns the Studio, manages revenues, and invests in real-world assets.

Two player experiences: The Game (Free-to-Play, no financial rewards) and the Tournament (100% competitive, fair-play, no RNG, free entry, with crypto rewards funded by the Holding’s interest).

Model advantages: Compliance (not gambling), Sustainability (rewards funded by tangible assets), Growth (increasing assets → increasing interests → increasing rewards), and Innovation (first model linking blockchain, gaming, and RWA in a virtuous cycle).
